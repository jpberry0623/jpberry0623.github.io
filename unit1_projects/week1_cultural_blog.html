Value- Accomplishment: 3, relationships: 4, Self-respect: 3, Honesty: 4, Recognition: 2, Helping other people: 4, Having a family: 5.

If I had to pick one value from this list that is most important I would choose "having a family." I consider everyone in my direct family along with all of my close friends as part of my family. When you have people who love, respect, and support you, everything else in life becomes easier. The good is better with people you love to share it with and the bad is more bearable with friends and family to support you. Money, security, health, and possession can all come and go, but friends and family are the essential part of life in which I invest.
